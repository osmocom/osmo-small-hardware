# Netgear GS305E mounting in 3U component carrier

This is a mechanical design to mount a Netgear GS305E switch in a  3U component carrier.

## face plate

6 TE wide face plate

* 128.4 x 30.22 mm
* 6 openings
* 567 mm total path
* 2mm (2.5mm) material


## mounting plate

* 100 x 90 mm
* 4 openings
* 417 mm total path
* 1.5 mm material
